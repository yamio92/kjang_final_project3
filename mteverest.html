The climb

The Clime

In 2016 I did something not many people have done. I climbed Mt Everest. People die attempting to do the same thing but you only live once. They do climbings in groups so I took some pictures to document. It took a week and it was the hardest week of my life. Way below freezing temperatures and little oxygen are just some of the hardships but making it to the top made it worth it.
